1.1 Business/ Product Goals

Purpose

  • Understanding the Business and product
  • What Problems the company is trying to solve
  • How do business gets benefit from this software
  • Do they have an existing system
  • Identify the Stakeholders
  • Identify the Risks

Methods/ Activities

  • Kick-off meeting with all the parties, Stakeholder interviews
  • Provide a PRD - Product Requirements document to Client and get Business Requirements
    • Review these documents with the business party to ensure accuracy and completeness.
  • Industry Trends
  • Competitor Analysis (Market Research)
    • Find Competitors (Local/Global) from Google search, ChatGPT
    • Find Strengths and Weaknesses in Competitor Websites/Apps (Manual + ChatGPT)
      • Ex: ChatGPT - "Summarize the Strength's and Weaknesses of each of these Apps/Websites"
  • Current website/application analysis (Manual + ChatGPT)

Outcome

  • Clear Understanding of Business Objectives - Business Goals, Success Metrics: Identification of key performance indicators (KPIs) and success metrics that will measure the product's impact on the business.
  • Market and Competitive Analysis
  • Target Audience and their needs, Pain points
    • Can create a "Success metrics-Primary" ex: Increase Revenue, Increase number of bookings etc.
    • Secondary Metrics to Reduce time taken to sell a product, use of free trial etc.
  • Business Requirements and Constraints - Functional Requirements, Technical and Operational Constraints
  • Stakeholder Insights
  • Strategic Recommendations - Opportunities for Innovation, Product Vision and Scope
  • Project Plan Outline - High-Level Roadmap of preliminary project plan or roadmap that outlines major phases, milestones, and timelines based on research insights. This could be a flow chart.

1.2 User Research and Goals

Purpose

  • Find Target Audience
  • To gather insights about users, their needs, goals, behaviors, and pain points.

Methods/ Activities

  • Surveys - Google Forms → Document Survey Insights
  • User Interviews
    • Invite potential users to the office and gather information
    • User Interview Scripts - To Gather Insights (Google Forms, Excel)

Outcome

  • Survey Insights
  • Creation of user personas based on research data. These personas represent key segments of the user base and help guide design decisions.
  • Representation of user needs, goals, and frustrations.
  • Write use cases on how users use the application.
